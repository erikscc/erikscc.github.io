YOUR RIGHTS AND OPTIONS

Opt-out of targeted advertising

You can opt out of interest-based advertising by adjusting your privacy settings in the app. You can opt out and delete your Personalized Ads data from our Ad partner here.

Additionally, you can opt-out of interest-based advertising in all mobile applications by checking the privacy settings of your Android or iOS device and selecting "limit ad tracking" (Apple iOS) or "opt-out of interest-based ads" (Android).

Opt-out of marketing emails

You may opt out of receiving promotional communications, such as marketing emails from us by following the instructions in such communications.

Access the personal data we hold about you

If you wish to access the data stored about you, please contact us to request it - see the contact information below.

Your other rights

You also have the right to object to how we use or share your data, and restrict how we use or share your data - please contact us via the details below if any of these concerns come up.

HOW DO WE PROTECT YOUR DATA

Security Safeguards

To help ensure a secure and safe player experience, we are continuously developing and implementing administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or against loss, misuse, or alteration.

Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you the Service. Note that if you ask us to remove your data, we will retain some of your data that is necessary for our legitimate business interests, such as to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

International Data Transfers

Our Service is global by nature and your data can therefore be transferred anywhere in the world. Because different countries may have different data protection laws than your own country, we take steps to ensure adequate safeguards are in place to protect your data as explained in this Policy. Adequate safeguards that our partners may use include standard contractual clauses approved by the EU Commission and the Privacy Shield certification in case of transfers to the USA.

AGE LIMITS

We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal data about or direct or target interest-based advertising to anyone under the age of 16. If you are under 16, please do not send any data about yourself to us, including your name, address, telephone number, or email address. No one under the age of 16 may provide any personal data. If we learn that we have collected personal data about a child under age 16, we will delete that data as quickly as possible. If you believe that we might have any data from or about a child under the age of 16, please contact us.

We do our best to enable persons under the age of 16 to play our games while disabling all personally identifiable data collection. There may be times when we need to collect data to provide a service such as an In-App Purchase, but by providing credit card details to make such a purchase we consider that a guardian giving consent.

If you have any questions or concerns please contact us. We are here to help! Games are a fun and great way for children to play and learn, we strive to provide a great and safe experience for people of all ages.
